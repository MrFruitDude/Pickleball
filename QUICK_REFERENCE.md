## üìù App Review Notes (For Apple Reviewers)

```
Rally Score is an Apple Watch-first pickleball scoring app with iPhone companion.

TO TEST THE APP:

1. APPLE WATCH SETUP (Primary Experience):
   - Open Rally Score on Apple Watch
   - Tap "Start Match" on the overlay
   - Enter team names (e.g., "Team Red" and "Team Blue")
   - Select scoring mode (recommend "Side-Out" for simplicity)
   - Tap "Start Match"

2. SCORING A MATCH:
   - Tap "Team A Wins Rally" or "Team B Wins Rally" to award points
   - Notice automatic service rotation (watch for "On Serve" indicators)
   - Try the "Undo" button to reverse the last point

3. GAME COMPLETION:
   - Continue scoring until one team reaches 11 points with 2+ point lead
   - Match will automatically complete and show winner overlay

4. MATCH HISTORY:
   - Swipe up on main watch screen
   - Tap "History" to view recently completed matches

5. IPHONE COMPANION (Optional):
   - Open Rally Score on iPhone
   - When watch starts a match, Live Activity appears on lock screen
   - Tap Live Activity to open full spectator screen

Please reach out if you have questions about pickleball scoring rules.
```
