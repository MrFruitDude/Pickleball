FAQ

Does it work without my iPhone nearby?

Yes. All scoring happens on the watch. Your iPhone companion syncs match history when it next connects.

Can I export match data?

You can export to CSV from the iPhone app or share a recap directly from the watch.

When will the app be available?

We are targeting a Fall 2024 release. Join the TestFlight beta through the App Store listing when it becomes available.
